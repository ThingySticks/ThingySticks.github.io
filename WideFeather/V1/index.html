Wide Feather Prototyping PCB

A Wide (100mm x 50mm) prototyping PCB for the Particle Mesh and Adafruit Feather devices.

Previous Next

Features

  • Breakout area either side of the Feather board.
  • Feather pins connected to breakout pads either side of the device.
  • 4x M3 mounting holes.
  • Excellent Feather pin function PCB silk screen.
  • 1206 pads for I2C pullups on the bottom.
  • 7x 1206 pads with wire connectors along both sides of the top edge for SMD LEDs, Resistors, Capacitrors..
  • Suitable for Adafruit feather and new Particle (Argon, Boron, Xenon) Mesh devices.

PCB Layout

Dimensions

  • Length: 100mm
  • Width: 50mm
  • Hole 1: 3.2mm at 4,4
  • Hole 2: 3.2mm at 96,4
  • Hole 3: 3.2mm at 96,96
  • Hole 4: 3.2mm at 4,96
